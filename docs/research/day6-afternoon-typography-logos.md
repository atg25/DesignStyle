# Typography as Graphic Art in MCM

## Core Principles
- Typography IS the graphic (no added decoration)
- HUGE headlines common (60-200px+)
- Bold, confident, unapologetic
- Generous negative space
- Asymmetric alignment

## Typography Styles

### Hero Typography
- Size: Very large (responsive 3-12rem)
- Weight: Bold/Black (700-900)
- Tight kerning OR very wide spacing
- Solid saturated colors or stark black/white
- Force line breaks for impact

### Overlapping Type
- Multiple words at different sizes
- Semi-transparent layers
- Creates depth and movement
- MCM used this sparingly

## Logo Design Principles

### Typography-Based Logos
- Clean sans-serifs (Helvetica, Futura, Univers)
- Often all-caps
- Wide letter spacing
- Geometric shapes as accents
- Monochrome or 2-color max

### Famous MCM Logo Styles
- **Helvetica logos:** American Airlines, BMW, Lufthansa
- **Futura logos:** Volkswagen, FedEx
- **Custom geometric:** Eames, Herman Miller

### Color in Typography
- Solid blocks of color
- High contrast (black/white, orange/teal)
- One accent color max
- No gradients or effects
