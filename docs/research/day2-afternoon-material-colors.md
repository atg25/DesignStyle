# Mid-Century Modern Materials & Colors

## Wood Tones

### Teak (THE MCM Wood)
- Golden brown #b8860b
- Natural oils (water resistant)
- Straight grain, medium-coarse texture
- Exotic import (Burma, Thailand, Indonesia)
- Famous pieces: Danish credenzas, Hans Wegner chairs

### Walnut (American Classic)
- Dark brown #5c4033
- Domestic USA, cheaper than teak
- Often has purplish undertone #4a3c31
- Famous pieces: Eames Lounge Chair, George Nelson credenzas

### Rosewood (Luxury)
- Deep burgundy-brown #65000b
- Exotic, expensive, now CITES protected
- Highly figured grain with purple undertones
- Famous pieces: Luxury Eames chairs, Danish cabinets

### Birch/Beech (Scandinavian)
- Light cream-yellow #e8d6a1 (birch), Tan #c19a6b (beech)
- Native to Nordic countries, maximizes light
- Bends easily when steamed
- Famous pieces: Alvar Aalto chairs, Series 7 Chair

### Oak
- Light brown #d4c4a8
- Traditional American wood, affordable
- Very prominent grain
- Less common in high design, more mass-market

## Upholstery & Fabrics

### Leather
- Cognac #9b4c3a (most iconic)
- Black #1c1c1c
- Develops beautiful patina over time
- Top-grain quality standard

### Wool
- Charcoal #36454f, Orange #ff6b35, Teal #1fb7c8, Mustard #c19a6b
- Textures: Boucle (nubby), herringbone, tweed
- Durable, takes dye well

### Naugahyde (Vinyl)
- Black #0a0a0a, Red #c1272d, Turquoise #40e0d0
- Cheap, easy care, space-age material
- Shiny surface, can crack with age

## Hard Surfaces

### Formica Laminates
- Solid colors: White, yellow #ffed4e, turquoise #00ced1, pink, green
- Patterns: Boomerang, Atomic/Starburst, Speckled
- Heat/scratch resistant, affordable
- Main use: Kitchen countertops, table tops

### Linoleum
- Black & white checkerboard (THE MCM kitchen floor)
- Red #C1272D, green #228B22, yellow #FFD700
- Natural material (linseed oil), eco-friendly

## Metal Finishes

### Chrome (THE MCM Metal)
- Silver #c0c0c0
- Shiny, reflective, modern
- Famous pieces: Eames Aluminum chairs, Barcelona base, Bertoia Wire chairs

### Brass (Warm Accent)
- Polished #b5a642, aged #8b7355
- More expensive, luxurious
- Common in: Light fixtures, hardware
- Revival in late period (1965-1970)

### Aluminum
- Silver #d7d7d7
- Lightweight, space-age
- Famous pieces: Eames Aluminum Group chairs

### Copper (Late Period)
- Orange-brown #b87333, patina green #8fbc8f
- Warmth, craft movement
- Usage: Light fixtures, decorative bowls

## Glass

### Clear
- Nearly invisible rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)
- Shows other materials

### Smoked (Very MCM)
- Gray #a9a9a9
- Sophisticated, mysterious
- Common in table tops

### Colored (Decorative)
- Amber #ffbf00, green #228b22, blue #4169e1
